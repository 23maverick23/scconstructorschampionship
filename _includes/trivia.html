Some Formula One Trivia

F1 cars can go 0 to 100 MPH back to 0 in under 4 seconds.

F1 cars can achieve up to 20,000 RPMs (normal cars can only achive around 6,000).
The average F1 driver loses 4 kgs of weight in a race.
F1 cars create enough downforce (3.5G) to technically drive upside down.
