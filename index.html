Some Formula One Trivia

The formula in the name Formula One refers to a set of rules to which all participants and cars must comply and was originally and briefly known as Formula A. The first official world championship race was held in 1950 at Silverstone.[1]

Another Fun Fact to Know and Tell

The oldest extant teams to have never changed names include Ferrari, McLaren, and Williams (the current Mercedes lineage actually dates back to Tyrrell, which rebranded a number of times as BAR, Honda, and Brawn).[2]
